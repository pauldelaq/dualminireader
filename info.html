DualMiniReader

DualMiniReader is a dedicated reading interface optimized for handling bilingual texts. Many language apps come jam-packed with numerous features. Such apps can be over-complicated and can sometimes have a steep learning curve. As an alternative, this platform offers a simple and intuitive interface for viewing and learning from bilingual reading content. Just select the languages you want and click on words to see their equivalent translations.

I hope you enjoy it!
